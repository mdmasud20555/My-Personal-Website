MY STORY

Born and raised in the small yet enchanting South Asian country of Bangladesh. I was born on May 3rd, 2003. I am very proud of being a member of my family.

My educational journey started at Dream Kinder Garden in Paragram, setting the stage for my academic path. Progressing through Paragram High School, these early years fueled my thirst for knowledge.

After completing high school, I delved into college life at Shaheed President Ziaur Rahman College, marking a phase filled with cherished memories, profound lessons, and personal growth. It was here that my passion for learning and exploration truly blossomed.

Now, I'm actively engaged in the dynamic field of Business Administration at Daejin University, South Korea. Known for my politeness and kindness, I believe in the transformative power of empathy, fostering strong relationships and shaping my life's approach.

Driven by a relentless motivation for success, I constantly set new goals, pushing my boundaries. Embracing the belief that learning about the world sparks astonishment and inspiration, my curiosity propels me to seek new knowledge and experiences.
